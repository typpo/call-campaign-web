About

Speak Louder was created by Ian Webster as a way to provide free organization and telephony tools for grassroots causes.

To get in touch, email

This service has its origins in the Call Congress phone number, 1-844-USA-0234, which automatically connects callers with their representatives, one after the other. It was originally launched on Ian's site and has facilitated thousands of phone calls for causes as varied as gun control, social justice, and airport noise abatement.

It's easy to feel helpless when watching the news or discussing problems in our country. Speak Louder is rooted in the belief that representative democracy can work. Even a single phone call to a Congressperson creates marginal change. Congressional offices routinely compile statistics on the calls of their constituents. If you believe strongly in something, we encourage you to take action to back it up.
